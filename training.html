Training approval
Paid trainings that use the Essense training budget require approval from your team lead. Here's how it works:
Gratis (free): no approval needed. Just do it and log it.

Betaald — own cost: you choose to pay yourself. No approval needed.

Betaald — budget: request goes to your team lead via the Essense training request process. Budget is shared across the team, so request early.

Webinars and conferences: same as budget-paid — request via team lead if there is a cost.
